About Black Panther

  • The Black Panthers name is T’Challa.
  • T’Challa is the King of Wakanda.
  • His panther suit is made out of the worlds strongest metal named vibranium.
  • One of Black Panthers foes is Killmonger, who is actually a long lost Wakanda royalty.
  • Another one of Black Panthers enemies is Ulysses Klaue.
